What is Legal Lingo?

Legal Lingo is the cobblestone upon which the road to the American Dream is built. The free web-based service allows users to anonymously translate tedious, long, extraneous legal documents.

Legal Lingo allows anyone , regardless of English-ability, to fully understand legal commitments before they're entered.

Further, users who don't need translation services can use Legal Lingo's summarization service, which highlights important keywords in documentation and shortens the time required by users to understand documents.
